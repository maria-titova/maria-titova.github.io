Welcome!

My name is Maria “Masha” Titova. I am an economic theorist interested in Information Economics, Political Economy, and Industrial Organization.

I am currently an Assistant Professor of Economics (primary) and Political Science (secondary) at Vanderbilt University.

Before moving to Nashville, I completed a Ph.D. at the UC San Diego Department of Economics in 2021. My advisors were Renee Bowen and Joel Sobel. I also hold an M.Sc. in Financial Economics and a B.Sc. in Applied Mathematics and Information Science from the Higher School of Economics in Moscow, Russia.

Here is my CV. You can reach me at maria.titova@vanderbilt.edu.
